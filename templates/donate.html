üí° Donation Tips

Check expiration dates: Only donate items that are within their expiration date or have reasonable shelf life remaining.


Consider dietary needs: Many families have specific dietary requirements. Items like gluten-free, low-sodium, or culturally appropriate foods are especially valuable.


Think about families: Items that can feed multiple people or be easily shared are particularly helpful for larger families.


Contact first: Before making a large donation, consider calling ahead to confirm what items are most needed at that time.
